# 💫 About Me:
I’m an Electrical Engineer bridging the gap between energy infrastructure and data-driven intelligence. Currently, I’m a Research Analyst Intern at ZeTheta Algorithms, where I develop quantitative simulations for financial markets, NLP-driven sentiment analysis for earnings calls, and predictive models for real estate cash flow stress. My technical foundation is rooted in Energy AI, with a portfolio ranging from feeder demand forecasting and smart building optimization to predicting battery State of Health (SoH) using NASA datasets. To further evolve my toolkit, I am currently deep-diving into Deep Learning and SQL while refining my core Python architecture. I am passionate about collaborating on open-source projects involving smart grids or renewable energy systems. Whether you want to discuss power system fault detection, multi-variable demand modeling, or my research on diagnostic AI, feel free to reach out—I’m happy to chat in English, Hindi, Bengali, or German!
